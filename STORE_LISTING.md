## Description (Full)

### Stop losing repeat sales.

40% of e-commerce revenue comes from repeat customers, but most stores do nothing to bring them back.

**ReStock Reminder** fixes that with zero effort. Set how often each product needs restocking (30 days for coffee, 60 days for skincare, 90 days for supplements), and we'll automatically email customers when it's time to reorder.

### How it works:
1. **Install** the app and your products are automatically imported
2. **Set intervals** — How many days until a customer needs more? (e.g., 30 days for coffee beans)
3. **Sit back** — We track every order and send reminder emails at the perfect time

### Why merchants love ReStock Reminder:

- **Set and forget** — Runs completely on autopilot after initial setup
- **Direct revenue impact** — Every reminder email is a potential sale
- **Customizable emails** — Edit subject line and body to match your brand
- **Per-product intervals** — Different products, different timing
- **Dashboard analytics** — See reminders sent, open rates, click rates
- **Works with any product** — Coffee, supplements, skincare, pet food, cleaning supplies, and more

### Perfect for stores selling:
- Food & beverages
- Health supplements
- Skincare & beauty
- Pet supplies
- Cleaning products
- Office supplies
- Any consumable or regularly-replaced product

### Pricing:
- **Free**: 50 reminders/month — perfect for getting started
- **Pro ($9.99/month)**: Unlimited reminders, 7-day free trial

### Privacy:
Customer data is used only for sending reminders. We never sell or share your data.
